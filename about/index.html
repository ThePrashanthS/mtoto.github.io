About

I am a Senior Data Analyst at ING. I received my Bachelor’s degree from KULeuven and my Master’s from the University of St. Andrews. My main area of expertise is digital analytics, however I have worked in a variety of industries such as Advertising, Aviation and most recently Banking. I love solving problems with data, discovering new insights and building scalable solutions.

While I do have a slight preference for R, I try to constantly learn and experiment with new technologies and frameworks. My other interests include getting lost in the Highlands, buying Blues CD’s and visiting Psytrance festivals.
